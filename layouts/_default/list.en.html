Aviator strategy and tactics

Players often try to minimize risks by developing numerous strategies and tactics for playing the Aviator game. Players use different Aviator strategies, which we will describe in the table below.

Martingale

With each loss, you need to double the bet. The bottom line is that players at the moment of winning will be able to compensate for all previous losses. It is important to understand that bettors should take into account their budget and have enough money to wait a long time for high coefficients.

2 by 2

Every 2 moves the player increases the bet if he wins or lowers it if he loses. The tactic is similar to Martingale tactic, and its essence lies in the fact that the game does not allow the player to lose for a long time and he will be able to take a sufficiently high coefficient finally.

Strict bet

Regardless of the course of the game, the participant places the same bet amount. Thus, the visitor does not lose large sums and can change the bet in the next session. Ideal for beginners and gamblers on a budget.

In order to choose the right playing strategy for Aviator, users should take into account their budget, time that players will spend playing the game, their experience and understanding of the game.
