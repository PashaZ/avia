Martingale

This is one of the most popular strategies for playing Aviator, which can be used not only for this game. Most often, players who are just starting to get acquainted with the Aviator choose the “One score” tactic. It implies that regardless of the loss or win, the next bet will be equal to the previous one. Thus, the player does not “shatter” his budget, he can afford both losing and multiplying the previous bet by more than x100.
