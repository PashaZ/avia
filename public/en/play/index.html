The essence of Aviator slot machine

The synopsis of the Aviator slot machine is as follows: the user predicts to what height the plane will be able to take off before it loses control. When the airplane rises into the air, the multiplier of the playerâ€™s bet raises. Before the plane gets out of control, you can claim your winnings by pressing the corresponding button at any time.

The playing area shows the runway of the airfield and the black sky above it. Below are two panels where you should bet. The left one indicates the size of the bet (it is set for the next round of the game), the right one indicates (optionally) the coefficient, upon reaching which the winnings are automatically withdrawn and credited to the player account.

When the player places bets, the airplane takes off. As it gains height, the coefficient grows (at the start, it is equal to one, which means the player will simply receive his bet back). If you do not press the withdraw button before the airplane flies away, the bet is lost and the round is considered lost.

How to play Aviator

After launching the Aviator slot machine, the user needs to place a bet. Before the start of another round, the random number generator determines the coefficient at which this round will end (visually, this is displayed by an airplane flying off the screen). After the start of the round, the plane takes off and gains height, while the coefficient increases in parallel with its rise. At any time, the players can click the "Cash Out" button, and then their bets will be multiplied by the current coefficient and credited to their account. At a random moment (determined by the RNG), the aircraft gets out of control and flies away. If the player has not withdrawn the winnings before it, his bet is lost and the round ends.

To place a bet, press the button "Bet". Optionally, you can make 2 parallel bets per one round. To do this, click on the button "Plus" on the bets panel in the upper right.

To withdraw your winnings, you must click "Cash Out". If the player manages to do this before the aircraft flies away, he will receive the amount of his bet multiplied by the coefficient of cashout.

Automatic playing and automatic withdrawal of winnings

The Aviator features an autoplay mode. Player can activate it in the menu on the control panel by clicking "Auto" button. After pressing it, in each next round, the same amount of money will be put on the stake.

You can withdraw winnings during regular or autoplay game manually using the "Cash out" button or automatically via "Autocashout" button. After clicking the latter, the winnings are withdrawn automatically when the coefficient reaches the user-specified level.
